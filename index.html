Oscar, My Dog

Dog type.

Oscar is an 18 month old german shepherd. He is a long coated GSD and is black in color.

Character.

Oscar, quite the dog, has the following traits:

  1. Loyal (Best attribute)
  2. Obedient
  3. Trained
  4. Guard dog
  5. Barks a few times
  6. Playful

Maintenance.

Feeding:

Oscar is fed once a day ,that is, in the evening. He is usually given rice and meat.
The meat can range from chicken heads and legs to others.
There should be a constant supply of clean water all day.

Activities

Oscar loves to do a lot of things:
  • Playing fetch
  • Constant walks
  • Running
  • Sniffing things

Bad habits.

Some of the bad habits of Oscar include;
  • Biting shoes
  • Barking loudly
  • Pouring its drinking water
  • Being too playful
